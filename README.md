
# 📊 Regional Sales Data Analysis & EDA

**Uncovering business growth and seasonal trends through data-driven insights.**

This project performs an end-to-end Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a complex Regional Sales dataset to evaluate true profitability, seasonal trends, and regional performance.

### 🚀 Project Highlights

* 🧹 **Comprehensive Data Wrangling:** Extracted and seamlessly merged relational data from multiple sources including Sales Orders, Customers, Products, Regions, and Budgets.


* ⚙️ **Strategic Feature Engineering:** Calculated `total_cost`, `profit`, and `profit_margin_pct` to shift the analytical focus from gross revenue to actual bottom-line profitability.


* 📅 **Time-Series Extraction:** Engineered temporal features (year, month, month name) to track business performance over time.


* 📈 **Actionable Visualizations:** Developed comparative line charts illustrating monthly revenue across different years, explicitly highlighting recurring seasonal demand and temporary slowdowns.



### 💡 Key Business Insights

* **Profitability over Revenue:** By calculating exact profit margins, the analysis identifies the true financial drivers across different products and regions rather than relying solely on surface-level sales.


* **Seasonal Optimization:** Identifying consistent high-performing and low-performing months allows the business to optimize inventory planning and launch highly targeted marketing campaigns during slow periods.



### 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Data Manipulation & Cleaning:** Python, Pandas, NumPy


* **Data Visualization:** Matplotlib, Seaborn
